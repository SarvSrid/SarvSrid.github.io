Welcome, Food Lovers!

Are you a culinary adventurer in search of the perfect meal on campus? Look no further! We've curated a list of the most exceptional dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From crispy chicken sandwiches to fiery wings, this guide has got you covered. Put on your foodie hat and dive into this delightful world of flavors! Get ready to discover the best food spots, all within a half-mile from Petty Building!

Chick-fil-A

Location: Close to campus. Hours: 8 AM - 10 PM

Specialties: Chicken Sandwich, Nuggets, Waffle Fries.

Buffalo Wild Wings

Location: Less than 0.5 miles. Hours: 11 AM - 12 AM

Specialties: Wings, Burgers, Fries.

Salsarita’s

Location: Right around the corner. Hours: 10 AM - 9 PM

Specialties: Burritos, Tacos, Bowls.

Tips and Promotions

  • Use Flex Dollars at Chick-fil-A and Salsarita’s!
  • Buffalo Wild Wings: 2-for-1 wings on Tuesdays!
  • Salsarita’s: Burrito special every Wednesday.
